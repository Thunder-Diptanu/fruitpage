1. Apple Description :


- Origin: Central Asia, now widely grown worldwide.

- Characteristics: Crisp, juicy, typically sweet or slightly tart . Apples come in a variety of    colors, including red, green, and yellow.

- Nutritional Benefits: High in dietary fiber, vitamin C, and antioxidants . Apples are low    in calories and promote heart health.

- Uses: Eaten raw, used in salads, desserts, juices, and sauces.





2. Banana Description :


- Origin: Southeast Asia and Pacific regions.

- Characteristics: Long, curved fruit with soft flesh inside a thick, yellow peel.

- Nutritional Benefits: High in potassium, vitamin B6, and dietary fiber. Bananas help    with digestion, energy production, and heart health.

- Uses: Eaten fresh, used in smoothies, baked goods, or dried as banana chips.





3. Strawberry Description :


- Origin: Native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

- Characteristics: Bright red, juicy, sweet fruit with tiny seeds on the surface.

- Nutritional Benefits: Rich in vitamin C, manganese, and antioxidants. Strawberries also    support heart health and skin care.

- Uses: Commonly eaten fresh, used in smoothies, jams, desserts, and salads.





4. Orange Description :


- Origin: Southeast Asia, but now cultivated worldwide in subtropical and tropical regions.

- Characteristics: Round, orange-colored citrus fruit with a tangy and sweet flavor.

- Nutritional Benefits: High in vitamin C, fiber, and antioxidants. Oranges promote    immune function   and skin health.

- Uses: Eaten fresh, juiced, used in desserts, and as flavoring for sauces and beverages.





5. Grapes Description :


- Origin: Near East, particularly the Mediterranean region.

- Characteristics: Small, round, and juicy fruits, often green, red, or black in color, growing    in clusters.

- Nutritional Benefits: Rich in vitamins C and K, and powerful antioxidants like    resveratrol . They support heart health and reduce inflammation.

- Uses: Eaten fresh, dried as raisins, made into juice or wine, or used in salads.





6. Mango Description :


- Origin: South Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia.

- Characteristics: Juicy, fleshy, and sweet fruit with a large pit inside. The skin color can    range from green to yellow, red, or orange.

- Nutritional Benefits: High in vitamins A and C, dietary fiber, and folate. Mangos are    great for digestion and immune support.

- Uses: Eaten fresh, in smoothies, salads, desserts, or dried and used in chutneys.
